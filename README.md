# cons1099
Consolidated 1099 Parser

[Document Grand Totals]
Compares the final totals for these lines from the form.
Total Dividends & distributions
Total Tax-exempt dividends
Total Foreign tax withheld

Total Dividends & distributions corresponds to boxes Boxes 1a, 2a, and 3
Total Tax-exempt dividends corresponds to Box 12

1a- Total ordinary dividends (includes lines 1b, 5, 2e)
2a- Total capital gain distributions (includes lines 2b, 2c, 2d, 2f)
12- Exempt-interest dividends (includes line 13)
